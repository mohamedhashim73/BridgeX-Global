# About 
BridgeX Global is an all-in-one digital platform designed to streamline hospitality, residential, and executive facility services. The app unifies everything guests and residents need into one seamless hubâ€”booking, service requests, maintenance reporting, visitor access, events, communication, and more. It delivers a smooth, secure, and modern experience tailored for premium environments.

# Key Features
- Secure Access
Login with OTP verification, manage your profile, and control account security with ease.
- Accommodation & Booking
Browse available rooms, book your stay, modify or cancel reservations, and add optional services directly during the booking flow.
- Food & Beverage Services
Explore menus, customize meals, place orders, track preparation and delivery, and book buffet or group dining services.
- Visitor Management (VMS)
Schedule visits, generate QR codes for fast, secure check-in/out, and manage visitor history.
- Maintenance & Facility Requests
Submit maintenance or cleaning requests, set priority levels, track progress in real time, and review past service records.
- Events & Activities
View upcoming community events, reserve tickets instantly, and receive QR e-tickets for entry.
- Directory & Vendors
Access a full directory of internal providers and nearby services with detailed info, contact details, images, and descriptions.
- Assets & Resources
Stay informed about assigned assets and manage items allocated to you.
- Communication & Feedback
Receive instant notifications, chat with administration, and rate completed services to improve overall quality.

# Technologies 
Dart, Flutter, BLoC/Cubit Pattern, Modular Architecture, RESTful APIs (Dio), Local Storage (GetStorage), Multi-Language Support, Firebase Messaging, Crashlytics.
